What is the H-120?

The Mindray H-120 Automated Glycohemoglobin Analyzer is a precision instrument used in clinical laboratories worldwide for HbA1c measurement. It employs Ion-Exchange HPLC technology to separate and quantify hemoglobin variants with exceptional accuracy.
